What is it and why is it better?

Dwolla is an incredibly low-cost payment network. Unfortunately, it's pretty painful to use in practice. You have to remember all of the shops who accept it. And you have to go through the song and dance of paying with your mobile phone. We solve that, using your existing card to pay through Dwolla (saving the fees).

One card, two networks

We'll automatically use your Dwolla account when you charge your existing credit card at participating merchants. Your card will function as normal at all other merchants. You get the low costs of the Dwolla with the ease of use of a credit card.

Safe and Secure

We never store your credit card number. We immediately encrypt it with a secure one-way hash function. Even we won't be able to retrieve the original card number once your account is setup. View the source code on Github.
