One card, two networks

We'll automatically use your Dwolla account when you charge your credit card at participating merchants. When they don't, your card will function as normal.

Link once, works always

Once you setup your credit card to link with your Dwolla account you're good to go. There's no longer any need to try to memorize all of the merchants who take Dwolla.

Safe and Secure

We'll never store your credit card number. We encrypt it with 2048-bit military grade encryption and throw away the key. Even we won't be able to retrieve the number once your account is setup.
